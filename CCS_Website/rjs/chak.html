The What ?

Chakravyuh is the most awaited event of Thapar Institute Of Engineering & Technology. CCS organizes this mega event every odd semester.Spanning over an entire week, the event gives the budding technocrats of TIET a chance to prove their mettle at the vanguard of technical competitions and further improve on their skills .
